Plays the sound item.

Parameters

$startTime:Number (default = 0) — The time, in seconds, to start the sound at (default: 0)
 
$loops:int (default = 0) — The number of times to loop the sound (default: 0)
 
$volume:Number (default = 1) — The volume to play the sound at (default: 1)
 
$resumeTween:Boolean (default = true) — If the sound volume is faded and while fading happens the sound is stopped, this will resume that fade tween (default: true)
